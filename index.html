Our Story

About 3D Sound

Founded in Toronto, 3D Sound began as a vision to elevate the way music and sound are experienced globally. What started as a passion-driven DJ collective has grown into a global entertainment brand, delivering curated sound, premium production, and unforgettable moments across weddings, private events, corporate functions, and international stages. Since it's inception in 2005, the 3D team has always maintained an emphasis on quality over quantity. When it comes to our clients' special moments we make no exceptions. We are committed to delivering nothing less than the premium sound experience.

At 3D Sound, music has no boundary — experienced around the globe — it's the heartbeat of every experience.
