Our Story

About 3D Sound

3D Sound began as a collective of like minded individuals from the streets of Toronto, Scarborough to be specific, looking to elevate the way they experienced music. What started as a passion project by some kids from the block has effectively grown into a global entertainment conglomerate.

Delivering curated premium sound experiences and unforgettable moments for weddings, private events, corporate functions or night life events – locally or internationally.

Since its inception in 2005, the 3D Family has always maintained an emphasis on quality over quantity. When it comes to our clients' special moments we make no exceptions. We are committed to delivering nothing less than THE PREMIUM SOUND EXPERIENCE.

At 3D Sound, music has no boundary — experienced around the globe — it's the heartbeat of every experience.
